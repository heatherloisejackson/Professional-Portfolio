About

Heather Jackson is an aspiring Web Developer located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. She graduated from Temple University in May of 2020, where she received a degree in Art History and Criticism. Throughout her undergrad, she gained experience in various dimensions of the art world- including fundraising and development, collections management, education, customer service, and administrative direction. Since graduating from Temple, Jackson has been working with small businesses to build their brands through various forms of marketing and gain virtual foot traffic by means of website design and e-commerce. She spends her days studying code through Penn’s Web Development bootcamp and her nights waiting tables on the rooftop of the historic Bok Building in South Philadelphia.
